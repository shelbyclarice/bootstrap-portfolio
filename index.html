About Me

Hi there! I'm Shelby, a web developer and designer based in South Florida.

I am a graphic designer with a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design and a Certificate in Web Development. My expertise blends creative design with technical web development, allowing me to create visually engaging and user-friendly websites.

I use Adobe Creative Cloud to create assets that improve both the look and function of websites. On the front end, I work with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, while my back-end skills include Python and PHP. I stay up-to-date on the latest coding and design trends to keep my projects fresh and effective.

I’m passionate about creating digital experiences that blend creativity with functionality. Currently, I am seeking opportunities to apply my skills in a collaborative environment where I can continue to learn and grow as a developer.
